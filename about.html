About the Program

The BS-MS in Mathematics and Data Science is a unique interdisciplinary program at HBTU designed to combine strong theoretical foundations in mathematics with modern computational tools and data science applications.

  • Duration: 5 years (BS + MS Integrated)
  • Core Areas: Linear Algebra, Probability, Statistics, Machine Learning
  • Tools: Python, R, SQL, TensorFlow,Power BI Tableau
  • Projects: Capstone, Research Projects, Internships
